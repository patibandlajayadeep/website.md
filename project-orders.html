Clinical Orders & Claims Optimization

Context 4 Healthcare · Orders, claims, and rules engine

This project focuses on how ordered services (such as labs, imaging, and procedures) are validated by a rules engine before claims are processed.

My Role

  • Worked with SMEs and billing staff to understand how different ordered services should be validated.
  • Reviewed real claims examples and identified patterns that needed rules or configuration changes.
  • Converted workflow requirements into clear rule configurations and test cases.

Key Responsibilities

  • Analyzed orders and claims data to define when a service should be allowed, denied, or flagged for review.
  • Configured rules to consider diagnosis codes, procedure codes, age, and other clinical factors.
  • Designed test scenarios (positive and negative) to verify behavior across typical and edge cases.
  • Executed regression testing after updates to confirm that existing customers were not impacted negatively.
  • Documented logic, dependencies, and known limitations so that other team members could understand the behavior.

Outcomes

  • Improved accuracy of order and claim validation for healthcare clients.
  • Reduced manual review by catching issues earlier in the workflow.
  • Provided clear documentation that helped support and QA teams reproduce and resolve issues faster.
